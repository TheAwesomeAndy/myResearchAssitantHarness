---
description: Draft and check the three-paragraph cover letter.
---

1. Draft a sober cover letter, at most one page, three paragraphs:
   (1) what the paper says, (2) why it matters, (3) why it fits this
   specific journal's ongoing conversation. No hype.
2. Save it to `submission/cover_letter.md`.
3. Run `python scripts/check_cover_letter.py submission/cover_letter.md
   --journal "<journal name>"`.
4. Fix every finding and re-run until it passes. STOP.
