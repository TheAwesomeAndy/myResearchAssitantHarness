description: Module 7 retrospective and self-improvement.
---

Close the loop after a drafting round or a review round.

1. `python scripts/self_improve.py`.
2. Review open memory: `python scripts/memory.py list --status open`.
3. For each recurring finding or reviewer echo, propose a concrete
   harness upgrade (a new forbidden phrase, a checklist item, or a config
   change). A mistake that reaches a reviewer twice must become a rule.
4. Record new `lesson` entries and resolve any addressed entries. Present
   the improvement report and STOP.
