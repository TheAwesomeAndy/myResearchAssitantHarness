# Cover Letter

Use when preparing a submission package.

## Procedure
1. Draft three paragraphs, at most one page: what the paper says, why it
   matters, why it fits this journal's conversation. No hype.
2. Save to `submission/cover_letter.md`.
3. `python scripts/check_cover_letter.py submission/cover_letter.md --journal "<name>"`.

## Verification
The gate passes: three substantive paragraphs, under the word cap, no
hype, journal named.

## Stop point
Present the letter and the passing gate output, then stop.
