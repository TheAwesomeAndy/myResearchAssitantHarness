# Amplification

Use only after the paper is accepted.

## Procedure
1. Confirm the exact publication date with the user.
2. Draft a layman press release, a social thread on the core utility, and
   prompts for a graphical abstract and a video or audio overview.

## Verification
Every asset is accurate, free of hype, and consistent with the paper's
actual claims.

## Stop point
Present the assets and stop.
