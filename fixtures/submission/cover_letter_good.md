Dear Editor,

We submit our manuscript "Robust EEG Encoding Under Subject Level Evaluation Constraints" for consideration. The paper presents a subject-level evaluation protocol that closes a leakage gap in affective computing and reports its exact failure thresholds under perturbation.

This work matters because widely used pooled cross-validation inflates reported accuracy through identity leakage, and practitioners deploying these models in clinical settings need evaluation that reflects real subject-independent performance. Our protocol exposes and corrects that gap.

The Journal of Robust Machine Intelligence has published sustained work on evaluation methodology and reproducibility, and our contribution extends that ongoing conversation with a concrete, reusable protocol. We believe it is a strong fit for your readership.

Sincerely,
The Authors
