# Claim policy

Every research statement must be traceable to evidence.

- Every quantitative claim maps to a source: a table-manifest entry
  (`scripts/check_table_manifest.py`), a script output, a figure source,
  or a citation.
- Numbers in the manuscript must match the numbers in the source files.
- Unsupported statements are risks and must be revised or removed before
  acceptance.
- Negative results and lower-than-baseline numbers are reported exactly
  as measured. Never massage data or invent benchmark superiority.
- Interpretation uses cautious semantics ("suggests", "indicates"),
  never "proves" (`claims.overclaim`, `lang.cautious`).
