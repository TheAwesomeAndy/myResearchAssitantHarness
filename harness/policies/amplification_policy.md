# Amplification policy

Post-acceptance outreach, accuracy over hype.

- Acquire the exact publication date from the journal before publishing
  anything.
- Assets: a layman-accessible press release, a social thread on the
  paper's core utility, and prompts for a graphical abstract and a video
  or audio overview.
- Every public claim must match a claim the paper actually supports. No
  overstatement, no marketing superlatives.
- Do not disclose embargoed content before the publication date.
