# Submission policy

- Venue selection: build the target list from journals appearing twice or
  more in the reference list; verify aims and scope, time-to-publication,
  and open-access options.
- Predatory screening: reject venues with unsolicited invitations, poor
  sites, opaque fees, or fabricated metrics.
- Blinding scrub (double-blind): remove author names, affiliations,
  acknowledgments, funding, self-revealing citations, identifiable
  repository URLs, emails, and ORCIDs (`blind.*`). Single-blind and
  camera-ready require the author block (`single.authors-required`).
- Statements: a visible data-availability and ethics statement is
  mandatory (`data.availability`, `ethics.statement`).
- Cover letter: three sober paragraphs, at most one page
  (`check_cover_letter.py`).
- Reviewer suggestions: draw from the reference list; hard-block
  co-authors, close colleagues, and same-institution names.
- Rebuttal: 7-day cooling-off; itemized, courteous, exact page and line
  numbers; no emotional language (`check_rebuttal.py`).
