# Privacy Policy

A harnessed project should keep private material out of generic templates and public commits.

High-risk material includes credentials, unpublished raw data, private local paths, student work, reviewer names, private manuscripts, and account exports.

Default handling:

- keep restricted material outside the repository;
- use local-only directories such as `data_local/`;
- scan commits before publication;
- replace private paths with stable project-relative paths;
- report any possible exposure as a blocking risk.
